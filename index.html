About the Journal

Official journal of the Society for Addiction Psychology (SAP), established in 2022. Peer-reviewed, quarterly publication focusing on substance use disorders and mental health. Ensures strict plagiarism limits (<20%). Publishes original articles, invited articles, reviews, case reports, opinions, and letters. ISSN 3107-8398.
