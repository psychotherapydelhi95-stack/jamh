Author Guidelines — JAMH

⬅ Submit Your Manuscript

1. Manuscript Categories

  • Original Articles (2500–4500 words)
  • Review Articles (3000–6000 words)
  • Case Studies (1500–2500 words)
  • Short Communications (1000–1500 words)

2. Formatting Requirements

  • Font: Times New Roman, 12 pt
  • Spacing: 1.5 line spacing
  • Margins: 1 inch on all sides
  • File format: DOCX or PDF
  • Number all pages

3. Manuscript Structure

  • Title Page (title, authors, affiliations, contact info)
  • Abstract (150–250 words)
  • Keywords (3–6)
  • Introduction
  • Methodology
  • Results
  • Discussion
  • Conclusion
  • References (APA 7th)

4. Citation Style — APA 7th Edition

  • All references must follow APA 7th style.
  • Examples:
    • Journal Article: Author, A. (Year). Title. Journal Name, volume(issue), pages.
    • Book: Author, A. (Year). Book Title. Publisher.

5. Plagiarism Policy

  • Similarity index must be below 10%.
  • Submissions above this limit will be returned.

6. Ethics and Approvals

  • Human subject research requires Ethics Committee approval.
  • Upload ethics letter with submission.
  • Include Conflict of Interest statement.

7. Cover Letter

  • Statement of originality
  • Confirmation that manuscript is not under review elsewhere
  • Names and contributions of all authors

8. After Submission

  • Authors receive automatic email confirmation.
  • Initial editorial review takes 2–4 weeks.
